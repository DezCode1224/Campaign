Campaign Finance Reform

Our democracy should work for the people, not special interests. I'm committed to getting big money out of politics and ensuring that every voice is heard, not just those with the deepest pockets.

Overturning Citizens United

I support a constitutional amendment to overturn Citizens United and restore common-sense limits on money in politics. Corporations are not people, and money is not speech.

Public Financing of Campaigns

I support public financing of campaigns to level the playing field and ensure that candidates can run for office without being beholden to wealthy donors. This would allow more diverse candidates to run and would make elections more competitive.

Disclosure and Transparency

I will work to strengthen disclosure requirements so that voters know who is funding political campaigns. This includes:

  • Requiring disclosure of all political spending
  • Closing loopholes that allow dark money
  • Ensuring timely and accessible disclosure

Limiting Super PACs

I support limits on Super PACs and other independent expenditure groups that allow unlimited spending in elections. These groups have too much influence and undermine our democracy.

Small Donor Matching

I support programs that amplify the voices of small donors by matching their contributions, making it possible for candidates to run competitive campaigns funded by regular people, not just the wealthy.
