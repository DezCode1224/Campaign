Economy, Jobs & Infrastructure

Building an economy that works for everyone means creating good-paying jobs, investing in infrastructure, and ensuring that working families can thrive. I'm committed to policies that grow our economy from the bottom up and middle out.

Creating Good-Paying Jobs

I will work to create and protect good-paying jobs in District 1 by:

  • Supporting small businesses and entrepreneurs
  • Investing in workforce training and apprenticeship programs
  • Promoting fair wages and worker protections
  • Supporting union rights and collective bargaining

Infrastructure Investment

Our infrastructure is the foundation of our economy. I will advocate for investments in:

  • Roads, bridges, and transportation systems
  • Broadband internet access for all communities
  • Clean water and wastewater systems
  • Public transit and sustainable transportation

Supporting Small Businesses

Small businesses are the backbone of our local economy. I will work to reduce barriers to starting and growing businesses, expand access to capital, and ensure fair competition.

Fair Trade and Economic Security

I support trade policies that protect American workers and create jobs here at home. I will fight for policies that ensure economic security for working families.
