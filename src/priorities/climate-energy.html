Climate & Energy

Climate change is the defining challenge of our time. We must act now to protect our planet for future generations while creating good-paying jobs in the clean energy economy.

Transitioning to Clean Energy

I support ambitious investments in renewable energy and infrastructure, including:

  • Expanding solar and wind power
  • Investing in energy storage and grid modernization
  • Supporting electric vehicle infrastructure
  • Promoting energy efficiency programs

Creating Green Jobs

The transition to clean energy can create millions of good-paying jobs. I will work to ensure these jobs are accessible to workers in District 1 and that we invest in workforce training for the green economy.

Environmental Protection

I will fight to protect our air, water, and public lands, and hold polluters accountable. This includes:

  • Strengthening environmental regulations
  • Protecting clean air and water standards
  • Conserving public lands and natural resources
  • Supporting environmental justice initiatives

Climate Resilience

We must prepare our communities for the impacts of climate change that are already happening. I will advocate for investments in climate resilience and adaptation measures.
